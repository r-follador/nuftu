How to trade and sell your NFT

Exhibit your NFT

Show your NFT off to the World!
After minting your NFT, it will be showcased on Nuftu (you will receive a link similar to https://nuftu.com/nft/0). Link your NFT on your virtual and real life social network, spread the word.

Sell your NFT

You can sell and auction your NFT on any Ethereum compatible market place. The most popular NFT marketplace is OpenSea and Nuftu fully supports its integration.

On your Nuftu NFT page, click at the bottom on the OpenSea link. This will open your NFT on OpenSea, a popular marketplace for NFTs.
To be able to put your NFT on sale at OpenSea, you need to follow a couple of steps:

  • If you have not done so, install MetaMask in your browser.
    MetaMask manages your private key in a safe way and helps to identify you as the owner on OpenSea. After installation, see here on how to add your private key to MetaMask.
    If you do not have a private key yet, one will automatically be generated for you while minting your NFT.
  • Go to OpenSea's listing of your NFT
    The link at the bottom your NFT on Nuftu will take you directly there.
  • Click on the Sell button and follow the instructions.
    You can choose to sell your NFT for a fixed price or auction it off.
Note You will need to add Ether to your account to pay for the gas price for successful sales. See here for more details on how this works.
